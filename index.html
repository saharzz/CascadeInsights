Sahar Zangeneh, PhD. Co-founder and Principal Consultant.

SSahar Zangeneh (she/her) is a (bio)statistician specializing in the design and analysis of experimental and observational studies, including complex sample surveys, individual- and community-randomized trials, and observational cohort studies. Her methodological work focuses on developing statistical methods for addressing missingness and non-random selection, particularly when missingness or selection depends on unobserved factors (missing not at random). Her collaborative research addresses population health and health equity across biomedical and epidemiological domains. She holds a faculty position at the University of Washington, where she teaches graduate courses and mentors students.

Ali Shojaie, PhD. Co-founder and Principal Consultant.

AAli Shojaie (he/him) is the Norm Breslow Endowed Professor of Biostatistics and Statistics at the University of Washington, where he serves as Interim Chair of the Department of Biostatistics. His research lies at the intersection of statistical machine learning, statistical network analysis, and applications in biology and social sciences. He is the founding director of the Summer Institute for Statistics in Big Data (SISBID) at the University of Washington and Lead of the Data Management and Statistics (DMS) Core of the UW Alzheimer's Disease Research Center (ADRC).

Statistical Consulting

Cascade Insights LLP provides specialized statistical consulting services on complex study designs and integration of generative artificial intelligence methods with traditional statistical approaches for design and analysis of complex data sets. This includes methodological guidance, statistical analysis, educational training, and technical support to academic, industry, governmental and non-governmental clients.

We are a boutique Statistical Consultancy firm based in Seattle WA, west of the Cascade Mountains.
